About Nkwane ASV

TRULY SOUTH
AFRICAN.

Nkwane ASV is an independent, wholly South African practice established and registered in 2007. Based in Pretoria, we have a clear focus on grassroots communities and the upliftment of previously disadvantaged South Africans.

We conduct our affairs with uncompromising quality, innovation, integrity, honesty and professionalism — building lasting relationships with every client we serve.

Vision

Upliftment of disadvantaged communities through quality, innovation and independence.

Mission

Affordable, client-oriented services empowering women and previously disadvantaged communities.

Philosophy

Serving people, planning with people, and building on what communities already have.

Standard

PSIRA Grade B (No. 621308). Minimum Grade 11 and 1 year experience for all officers.

Events Security

UNIVERSITY OF PRETORIA
SPORTS EVENTS.

Since 2020, Nkwane ASV has been an active Event Security provider at the University of Pretoria, operating under the Fidelity Security Services framework at the Hillcrest Sports Campus (LC de Villiers).

We provide crowd control, access management, and safety coordination across a wide range of high-attendance sporting codes — from Varsity Cup rugby to national-level hockey tournaments. Our approach is grounded in formal risk assessment, SHE compliance, and military-disciplined operational protocols.

Every event is treated as a unique security challenge. Officers are briefed on site-specific access points, spectator flow, emergency evacuation routes, and crowd behaviour management per event.
